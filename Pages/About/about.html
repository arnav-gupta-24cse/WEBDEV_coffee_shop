Our Story

At Maison du Café, our journey began with a simple passion: to craft exceptional coffee experiences that bring people together. Founded in 2020 by Arnay, Advit, and Aaryaman, three coffee enthusiasts with a shared dream, our café has grown from a small local spot to a beloved community hub. We believe in the power of a great cup of coffee to start the day, fuel creativity, and foster connections. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community is at the heart of everything we do.

Ethical Sourcing

At Maison du Café, we are dedicated to ethical and sustainable coffee sourcing. We collaborate with small-scale farmers who uphold our values, ensuring fair compensation, responsible agricultural practices, and respect for the environment. Every bean is thoughtfully selected and roasted in-house to highlight its distinctive flavors and aromas. By choosing Maison du Café, you become part of a community that supports passionate coffee artisans and contributes to the growth of sustainable farming worldwide.
